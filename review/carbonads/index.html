Carbon Ads

Carbon Ads is an invite-only advertising network that helps developers and creators monetize their traffic with safe ad units for users and respect their privacy. Carbon Ads was created to address these problems. They are also lightweight and do not slow down the user’s device.

How to Make Money with Carbon Ads?

Carbon Ads has some requirements then it comes to accepting new sites into the networks. Based on their FAQ, they will look into several factors like:

  • The relevancy of your site to their target audience.
  • The number of your site monthly page views.
  • The activity of your site.

It boils down to whether they find your site is a good fit to the networks.
